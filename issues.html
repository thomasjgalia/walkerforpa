Joe's Platform

The priorities Joe will champion in Harrisburg from day one.

📈

Economic Opportunity

Creating good-paying jobs, supporting small businesses, and ensuring working families can make ends meet. Joe will fight for fair wages and economic policies that build a stronger middle class.

🏫

Education

Every child deserves a quality public education regardless of zip code. Joe will work to increase school funding, reduce class sizes, and ensure our teachers are paid what they deserve.

🩹

Healthcare

Healthcare is a right, not a privilege. Joe will fight to lower prescription drug costs, protect coverage for pre-existing conditions, and expand access to mental health services statewide.

🌿

Environment

Pennsylvania's natural resources are a legacy worth protecting. Joe supports clean air and water standards, investing in clean energy, and building a green economy with good jobs for Pennsylvanians.

🏠

Safe Communities

Every Pennsylvanian deserves to feel safe at home and in their community. Joe will support smart public safety investments, address gun violence, and strengthen community policing partnerships.

⚖

Good Government

Harrisburg should work for the people, not special interests. Joe will push for transparency, ethics reform, and accountability so every constituent's voice is heard.
