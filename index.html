About Joe

A Pennsylvania Leader
You Can Trust

Joe Walker is a lifelong Pennsylvanian who understands the challenges facing everyday families. He has spent his career standing up for workers, advocating for strong public schools, and fighting to ensure every community has access to the resources it needs to thrive.

Joe believes that good government means showing up — listening to constituents, being accountable, and putting people before politics.
